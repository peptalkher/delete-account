Delete Account Request

To delete your account and associated data, please follow these steps in the app:

  1. Open the app and log in. Tap on the profile icon at the bottom right corner of the screen to go to the "Profile" section.

  2. Under "Profile", scroll down and tap on "Delete user".

  3. Enter your password to confirm and tap "Delete user".

If you have any questions or issues, please contact our support team at hello@peptalkher.com.
